# Ashes of the Scroll: The Trial of Fortitude

**Ashes of the Scroll** is a 3D meditation on spiritual discipline and the virtue of fortitude. It invites the seeker to navigate a digital landscape where every movement is an act of will and every challenge is a test of character.

### Spiritual & Technical Architecture
- **The Virtue of Precision**: Implements a frame-perfect hit detection system where success is found not in aggression, but in the temperance of one's actions.
- **The Path of the Pilgrim**: Features a "Perfect Roll" system, symbolizing the grace required to navigate the trials of the world without being overcome by them.
- **Ordered Adversity**: Challenges are driven by a sophisticated state-machine (Patrol, Alert, Chase), representing the predictable yet relentless nature of the spiritual struggle.
- **Illumination & Shadow**: Custom particle emitters and cinematic camera lerps create an atmosphere of sacred awe, reflecting the beauty found in the struggle for truth.
